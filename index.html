Amalfi Coast

#1 in Best Beaches in Italy

"One look at the Amalfi Coast and you may believe that you've found heaven on earth- Located in the Campania region of Italy, this UNESCO World Heritage Site covers 34 miles of majestic terrain; sky-high costal cliffs display vibrant vegetation and multicolored towns live side by side with the disarming turquoise waters of the Mediterranean, creating a scene that has the power to stop even the most seasoned of travelers dead in their tracks."


Things To Do:

  • Diving
  • Swimming
  • Hike the Path of Gods
  • Day trip to Island of Capri
